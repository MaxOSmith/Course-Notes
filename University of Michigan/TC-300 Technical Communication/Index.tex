\abstr{This course is designed to further your education in technical communication in a way that will be valuable to you in both your coursework and your careers. I’ll employ a series of lectures, workshops, and discussions to help you learn principles and conventions of technical writing, research, and communication. During the semester, you’ll complete a job unit, a presentation unit, and a research unit. You’ll produce both individual work and teamwork, and you’ll have practice putting what you learn to use. }
